About Me

I am a 4th semester (Bachelors) student at Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology, Burla pursuing Information Technology. I am very passionate about Open Source and everything that’s related to tech. I love making new things, and coming up with solutions to challenging problems. I thrive in a competitive environment and create a plan to complete my tasks within deadline. I like to give 100% of my efforts into things I am working on, so that later I can look at it, and say ‘I did it’. It ensures that I progress through my task smoothly towards getting completed. I am always open to learning new things, new technologies which look interesting.

My technical knowledge is in C++, Python, Django, Node Js and Deep Learning. I have taken up many online courses to gain more knowledge and build projects using the knowledge I gained. Open Source really motivates me because I get an exposure to people who love working with technologies like me, and coming up with a solution to challenges, and create something new, which will get used by thousands of people everyday, and I will be proud if someone uses the code I have written for their help.

I am also exploring Backend development technologies like Express Js, Graph ql, and APIs.

Aside this, I love watching and playing Football, I like to keep up with business news and entrepreneurship. Whenever I need a break, I go out and play football, and watch Football matches late at night, supporting my team, Manchester City. I guess it describes me as a person, involved in team activity, happy at what I am doing, and always curious to know new things.
